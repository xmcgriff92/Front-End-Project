Our online library provides access to more than 3.2 million physical and digital volumes, 159,714 physical and digital journals and other serial subscriptions, 108,834 e-journal titles, 724,075 e-books, and 419 databases.

Our extensive variety of information available advances student success, knowledge creation and preservation, and globally competitive research. We are proud to provide our community with the nessesary resources they may need reagardless of situation.
